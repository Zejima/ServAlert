The Problem

The Google Nest Protect is an IoT smoke alarm device. It is capable of measuring the presence of carbon monoxide in the air and sending messages to the usrs' phone in case of fire. However, this device is costs 119, not including extra proprietary products$

Our Solution

Using an MCU coupled with a smoke sensor and a IoT Sim it is possible to create a low-cost, low-power alternative to the Google Nest protect and act as a new avenue in the field of internet connected alarm devices.

Design

Our design in cludes the Nordic 9160 development kit which comes with a Segger Jlink to program the MCU. Coupled with it is a Arudino smoke shield and a Twilo Super Sim to connect to the Twilio API to send a message to the user's phone.
